My way to success

I graduated from the Faculty of Contemporary Literature and had a master's degree in contemporary image at the Fondazione Fotografia of Modena.

I started taking pictures when I was a child, and I mainly worked on portraits and body language.

I held exhibitions all over Italy and Europe, and in addition to art projects, I worked in the fashion industry with editorials, advertising campaigns and music videos.
